We believe that we can save more lifes

Open Door Mission is one of the leading organizations that empowers young people to work on their Health, heal and mend their hearts, reconcile families and counsel and advise Youth on how to live a success life. We teach, train, connect and financially support youth and widows from different backgrounds to actively participate in prevention programs, practice and exercise their rights, do research, policy and leadership on Youth Health.

Open Door is one of the largest global chrity community in the world

The open Door Mission was officially established in 2015 as a small Youth Uniting Network (YUN) with Dr.Eric as the head Founder and was connecting youth organizations to work towards the prevention of idolism and reduction of poverty among them. Then later on the elder council was evolved as the youth needed guidance and counselling services of elder mentors.
